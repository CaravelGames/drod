Mod management screen

This screen shows a list of game media that are installed. The list defaults to the set of pre-defined room styles.

Export: You may make your own style mods (documented elsewhere). When your graphical mod is ready, click Export to package your image files into a .DRMF (DROD Media File) on disk. You may opt to publish your DRMFs on CaravelNet for other DROD players to download and use in this game.

Import: Select a .DRMF to install a mod file you have on disk to your DROD installation. (Doing this will insert an entry for the mod in your drod.ini file.)

Download: With a CaravelNet subscription, you may download and import graphical mods published on CaravelNet.

Filter: By default, all visible mods are listed. You may select to view only CaravelNet or local mods.

Delete: Select a mod that you have imported previously and click Delete to remove it from your installation.
