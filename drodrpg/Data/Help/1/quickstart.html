Quick Start & Keyboard Commands

This is a game of strategic dungeon exploration. Guide Tendry in becoming an adept swordsman by fighting monsters and solving puzzles.

Here are some instructions for people who don't like to read instructions.  To advance in this game:

1. Find keys to open doors.
2. Acquire weapons, armor and power-ups to become stronger and defeat enemies in your way.
3. Find the exit to the next level.

You may use the mouse or keyboard to play. To move with the mouse, just click on the square you wish to move to. Tendry will walk there if there is a direct path. Click past the edge of the room to move into the next one. If you have a mouse wheel, it can be used to rotate Tendry.

Keyboard commands available during the game are:

7 - move northwest 8 - move north 9 - move northeast
4 - move west 5 - wait 6 - move east
1 - move southwest 2 - move south 3 - move southeast
 
q - swing sword counter-clockwise
w - swing sword clockwise
r - restart the current room
Backspace - undo moves made in this room
+ (keypad) - View stats for monsters in the room
- (keypad) - View your score, and how it is calculated from your current statistics
0 (keypad) - Lock door at this tile
. (keypad) - Special command (when active)
Tab - Use equipment

These keyboard commands can be changed from the Settings Screen. For more information and other game commands, see Game Commands.

Hints: This is primarily a puzzle game. In order to succeed, you must use strategy to carefully pick your battles. Don't fight hard monsters before you're prepared, or you might find yourself too weak to continue. If you can avoid monsters, it's probably best to not fight them at all.

If you're stuck, check out the Caravel Forum for gameplay hints and solutions.
