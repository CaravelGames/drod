The Story

Tendry is a member of the Stalwart Army from the grand city of Tueno. In DROD RPG: Tendry's Tale, the Empire that resides underground came to the surface and kidnapped his countrymen!

Tendry founded the Stalwart Delving Corps to establish a team to travel beneath and rescue the captive Tuenan citizens. Unfortunately, the Empire is large, and their network of tunnels very complex, so progress was slow.

Tendry is not alone in the beneath. His previous encounters with Beethro Budkin, a Smitemaster from Dugandy, proves how far-reaching the Empire's tunnel network goes. And of course, who can forget his most faithful compainion, Roachie, Tendry's pet roach.

Join Tendry as he uncovers the mysteries of the Beneath!
